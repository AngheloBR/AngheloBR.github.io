About me

I'm Jaren Bailon, a Networks & Cybersecurity graduate from SENATI (Peru). I run my own Linux homelab, break things on purpose, and write it down so the next person doesn't have to.

My focus is Linux server security: SSH hardening, firewall configuration, automated updates, intrusion prevention. Not flashy work — boring, reliable, documented.

I keep this site as a public log of what I learn. If you need someone to harden your VPS without overselling, you're in the right place.
